## How to Log In

To access the internal apps, follow these steps to log in:

### Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page

<a href={site_url} target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Click here first</a> to access the login page:

![Login Page](/images/authentication.png)

### Step 2: Microsoft Login

After clicking on the login page, you will be redirected to the Microsoft login screen. Enter your Microsoft credentials:

![Microsoft Login Screen](/images/login_screen.png)

### Step 3: Post-Login

Once you've successfully logged in with Microsoft, you should see a page that looks like this:

![Post Login Page](/images/post_login.png)

You're now logged in and can access all available features.
