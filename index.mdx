### Repairs

Manage customer-ordered repairs and track them from initiation to completion.

<Card
  title="Repairs Overview"
  icon="wrench"

>
  Learn how to create, view, and manage repair orders. Available to all logged-in users with different views based on user role.

### DC Orders

Order DC (Distribution Center) items for your store location.

<Card
  title="DC Orders Overview"
  icon="shopping-cart"

Request marketing materials and supplies for your store location through a customizable form system.

<Card
  title="Marketing Supplies Overview"
  icon="file-pen"

View contact information for all stores and home office employees.

<Card
  title="Phone Book Overview"
