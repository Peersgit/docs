## Viewing Responses

After clicking the "View Response" button on the marketing-materials/admin page, you will see the fulfillment and export interface:

![Fulfillment and Export](/images/fullfillment-and-export.png)

## Available Actions

### Export Responses

You can export all responses to the form that have ever existed.

### Mark Requests as Complete

In the table, you can select a row and mark it as complete. When fulfilling a marketing request, you can check items off one at a time.
