## Purpose

Request marketing materials and supplies for your store location through a customizable form.

## Access

**All logged-in users** can access the marketing supplies form to submit requests.

**Home office staff in the Marketing department** have additional access to:
- Edit and update the form structure
- View and manage form responses
- Fulfill marketing requests
