## How to Create a New DC Supplies Order

Follow these steps to create a new DC Supplies order:

### Step 1: Go to Orders Page

Navigate to the DC Supplies Orders page:

<Card
  title="Access DC Supplies Orders"
  icon="external-link"
  href={`${site_url}/store-supplies/dc-orders`}
  target="_blank"
  horizontal
/>

![Orders Page](/images/orders.png)

### Step 2: Click New Order

Click the "New Order" button to begin creating your order:

![Make Order](/images/make_order.png)

### Step 3: Search and Add Items

Search for the items you need and click and drag them into the empty container on the right side of the screen:

![Item Search](/images/item-search.png)

![Click and Drag](/images/click-drag.png)

### Step 4: Review and Create Order

Review your order to ensure all items are correct, then create the order:

![Order Review](/images/order-review.png)

### Step 5: Order Created!

Congratulations! Your order has been successfully created.
