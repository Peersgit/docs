## Store Phone List

You can search and view all stores. Here you can find:
- Store address
- Extension and phone number
- GL accountant and their email
- Store leadership team including all ASMs, managers, and leads with their extensions

## Home Office Phone List

You can search all home office employees by name, job title, or department. Here you can view:
- Profile picture
- Department
- Job title
- Extension and phone number
- Email address

**Additional Actions:**
- Click the email icon to send them an email
- Click the Teams icon to send them a Teams message
